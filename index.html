About The Chickeness

Founded on a lifetime of animal love

"I got my first chicks in May 2009 and never looked back."

Khrysti Smyth has 5 years as a veterinary technician and years of wildlife ecology fieldwork behind her. She helped shape Somerville's first urban agriculture ordinance in Massachusetts — and has been connecting urban chicken keepers with expert guidance ever since. Based in Granby, MA, serving all of New England.
