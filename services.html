Flock care

Keeping your girls healthy & happy

On-site wellness services currently available within 30 min of Granby, MA or Northampton, MA. Khrysti is not a veterinarian — for serious illness, she'll always tell you to see a vet.

Coop cleaning visit

Full bedding removal and replacement with fresh pine shavings, light run tilling, and a check for any minor maintenance issues — loose wires, signs of pests or predators.

$75/visit · or $50 when added to chicken sitting

Flock health & wellness check

Thorough assessment of overall flock health and behavior. Each bird checked individually for parasites or other issues. Nail and beak trimming as needed. Recommended at least once a year, ideally in the fall.

$100/visit (up to 6 birds) · $150 for 6–15 birds

$75/$100 when added to chicken sitting

Deworming — Wazine

Two visits ~24 hrs apart. YBC recommends deworming once every year or two in the fall, ideally when your birds are molting. Discard eggs for 2–4 weeks after treatment.

$45/treatment · or $25 when added to sitting (2-day min.)

Deworming — Ivermectin

Per-bird treatment, can be combined with a flock health check for significant savings since Khrysti will already be handling each bird.

$45/visit + $5/bird

$25 + $5/bird with sitting · $10 + $2/bird when added to wellness check

Sick bird triage

Quick remote call to help assess what's going on with an ailing hen. If it's serious, Khrysti will be honest and tell you to get to a vet — but she can often help you figure out what you're dealing with first.

$25 minimum donation · remote only
